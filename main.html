Laughter is contagious.


A bad day gets better by experiencing laughter.


Cozy laughters, weird laughters, sweet laughters and silly laughters. Even how irritating we can find other people’s laughter when we’re upset, it still makes us crack a smile.


It is also good for your health in the long run. A contagious laughter does well for mood, spirit and health. With this project we hope to spread the laughter and make people smile.


Share it with someone you think needs a boost or save it for the future.


We hope it makes you smile.
